PROJECT

THIS PROJECT WAS BASED ON MY FAVORITE ANIMAL THE OPOSSOM. I FIND IT UPSETTING THAT NOT MANY SEE THE BEAUTY THAT I SEE IN THEM. I DEDICATED THIS EXHIBIT TO THE VIRGINIA OPOSSOMS AND I USED THE LOVE AND PAIN I FELT FOR THESE CREATURES TO INSPIRE MY WORK. I DID 6 LARGE PAINTINGS ON CANVAS DOING ABSTRACT DEPCITIONS OF THE ANIMAL WITH HEAVY BRUSHSTROKES AND CONTINUED PAINTING OVER IT UNTIL THE CANVAS HAD LARGE CLUMPS OF PAINT IN AREAS.
BORGIA 2018, PAINT ON CANVAS
